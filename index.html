About

Ekaterina Dymova is licensed professional mediator and psychotherapist. Certificated in 2012, Ekaterina has more than 15 years of experience helping individuals find their inner strenght and clarity. As a dedicated advocate for mental health and well-being, Ekaterina is committed to empowering clients to overcome obstacles and live fulfilling lives. With a compassionate and client-centered approach, Ekaterina fosters a supportive therapeutic environment where individuals can explore their emotions, gain insight into their challenges, and develop practical skills for lasting change. With a deep understanding of human behavior and a passion for helping others, Ekaterina strives to create meaningful and transformative experiences for every client.

Counseling Offered

Invidiual Counseling

Receive personalized support and guidance in a safe and confidential environment, tailored to your unique needs and concerns, to help you navigate life's challenges and achieve personal growth and well-being.

Group Counseling

Join a supportive and collaborative group setting where you can explore shared experiences, gain insights from others, and develop new coping strategies and skills in a compassionate and non-judgmental atmosphere.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy

Explore the connection between thoughts, feelings, and behaviors to identify and challenge negative patterns, learn effective coping techniques, and cultivate healthier ways of thinking and relating to yourself and others.
